Ugly 10.20.2022

You look so ugly when you cry. It’s the whimpering that gets me. It's horrible. You’ve been strong for so long. I learned you hate being weak in front of people. Weakness is shame. It’s something you picked up growing up. You’re hard on yourself. I used to wonder why. I asked you if it was an issue of assurance. You told me it wasn’t. I believed you. You look so ugly when you lie. It’s trust in yourself. Security in knowing how beautiful a person you are, at your highs and at your lows. This wisdom I grasped the day I met you. Look in the mirror because sometimes you forget it. Look in my eyes and listen because sometimes you need to hear it. You are sublime.
